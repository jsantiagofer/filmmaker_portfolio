About

I’m a director, cinematographer, and editor based in Puerto Rico. My work focuses on the exploring identity, memory, and the human condition within the Puerto Rican context. I’m drawn to grounded, character-driven stories and a visual style that balances intimacy with bold composition and metaphorical storytelling.

Skills include: Sony FX30/FX3 workflows, BlackMagic Cinema Cameras, camera assistance, DIT, DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, producing, communication, and set leadership. Equipment ready.
